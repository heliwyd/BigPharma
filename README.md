# Big Pharma

A medical-themed expansion mod for *Software Inc* that adds new categories of theoretical medical software, medical devices, and pharmaceutical drugs.

The mod expands the in-game software market with structured medical industry product types and progression paths.

---

## Overview

Heli's Big Pharma introduces new software categories inspired by the healthcare and pharmaceutical industries.

All content represents **in-game software products**, not real-world systems.

The mod focuses on expanding industry variety and progression within Software Inc.

---

## Included Content

### Medical Software
- Patient Intake Software
- Research and Development Software
- Compliance Software

### Medical Devices
- Cat-Scan Machines
- X-Ray Machines
- MRI Machines

### Pharmaceutical Drugs
- Painkillers
- Antibiotics  
- Anti-Depressants
- Vaccines
- Experimental Drugs 

---

## Progression

### Early Game
- Basic medical software 
- Entry-level medical equipment

### Mid-Game
- Expanded medical and device systems  
- Expanded medical equipment

### Late Game
- Experimental Drugs (get caught skipping human trials!)

---

## Design

The mod is structured around a progression system that introduces medical software in increasing complexity over time.

Software types are designed to represent different sectors of the medical technology industry within the game.

---

## Summary

Big Pharma adds a structured set of medical-themed software categories to *Software Inc*, allowing players to develop companies focused on:

- Medical Software
- Medical Device Manufacturing
- Pharmaceutical Drug Manufacturing
